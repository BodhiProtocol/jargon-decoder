# Capital Markets Jargon Decoder

**Jargon is a wall. This is a door.**

Live demo → https://bodhiprotocol.github.io/jargon-decoder/

---

## Why this exists

Every industry protects itself with vocabulary. Capital markets more than most: novation, DvP, corporate actions, golden source, T+1.

This is 80+ of those terms decoded into plain language — one italic sentence anyone can understand, plus one more sentence of depth for those who want it.

Business analysts live at the translation layer between business and technology. This tool is that skill, made visible.

## What's inside

Six categories, mirroring the [Trade Lifecycle Explorer](https://github.com/BodhiProtocol/trade-lifecycle-explorer):

`Lifecycle · Clearing & Settlement · Instruments · Risk & Compliance · Market Structure · Technology`

Features: live search across terms and definitions, category filters, a **Surprise me** button for wandering, **mini-diagrams** for the ten most spatial concepts (novation, netting, DvP, T+1, bid-ask spread, ex-date, order book, options, circuit breakers, STP — look for the ◆ visual badge), and a **Test me** flashcard mode: read the plain-language definition, guess the term, reveal.

## Tech

One self-contained `index.html`. No frameworks, no build step, no dependencies, no API. Vanilla JS. Open the file, it works — even offline.
